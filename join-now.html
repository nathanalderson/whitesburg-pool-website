Join Now!

When you join Whitesburg Pool, you're not just paying to get in: you're joining a community! For over 50 years, families in South Huntsville have gathered at Whitesburg Pool to celebrate summer and form lasting memories together. Join us today and become part of that continuing tradition!

To join the pool, you must purchase a membership (a one-time purchase) and pay annual dues. The table below gives more details on pricing. Do note that a late fee of $50 will apply to dues paid after May 10th, 2019, or $150 if paid after June 1st.

New members must also submit this membership form (also availabe at the pool office). The form only needs to be submitted once unless your information changes. The form can be printed and brought to the pool office or mailed to 610 Sanders Rd., Huntsville, AL 35802.

Payments may be made online by purchasing the correct items via PayPal below. Clicking "add to cart" below will open a new tab with your PayPal shopping cart. Note that you can close the new tab to come back and add multiple items before checking out with PayPal. Alternately, cash or checks can be brought to the pool office or mailed to 610 Sanders Rd., Huntsville AL 35802.
