Join Now!

When you join Whitesburg Pool, you're not just paying to get in: you're joining a community with a tradition of summer memories reaching back over 50 years! Since 1962, families in South Huntsville have gathered at Whitesburg Pool to celebrate summer together. Join us today and become part of our continuing legacy.

To join the pool, you must purchase a membership (a one-time purchase) and pay annual dues. Annual dues are due by May 1st. The table below gives more details.

Membership $300 $50 2018 Special! One-time purchase. Annual dues also required (see below).
Trial Membership + Dues $200 Try out the pool for one summer. May only be purchased once. No additional dues for the first summer.
Swim Team Pool Fee $100 Pool use fee for swim team members who are not full pool members. Fee is paid annually and covers an entire family*.
Dive Team Pool Fee $50 Pool use fee for dive team members who are not full pool members. Fee is paid annually and covers an entire family*.
Individual Dues $309 Annual dues for an individual.
Family Dues $465 Annual dues for a family*.

* A "family" includes immediate family and other individuals living in a single household.
