Whitesburg Tide Swim & Dive Teams

Any area youth from age 4 to 17 are invited to join the Whitesburg Tide swim and dive teams! We're part of the Rocket City Swim and Dive League and swimmers and divers of any skill level are welcome. Whether you're super competitive or just learning, our awesome coaches will have you setting PRs in no time... all while having fun with your friends at the pool!

Questions?

Email swimteam@whitesburgpool.org

 

How to Join

Swim team registration for the 2023 season will open on May 1st. For questions about swim team, or to be added to the mailing list, please email swimteam@whitesburgpool.org. Fees

Swim team fees include t-shirts as shown below. Dive fees do not include a t-shirt. Swim caps, and extra shirts can be purchased at an additional cost if desired.

If you aren't a full pool member, you'll need to purchase a swim/dive team membership in addition to the team fees shown below. However, consider getting a trial membership instead, which will allow your whole family to come to the pool all summer long.

Swim Team

  • 1 swimmer: $120 (includes 1 t-shirt)
  • 2 swimmers: $190 (includes 2 t-shirts)
  • 3 swimmers: $250 (includes 3 t-shirts)
  • Each additional swimmer after 3: $15 (includes t-shirt)

Dive Team

  • 1 diver: $80
  • 2 divers: $105
  • 3+ divers: $125

Swim Team Schedule

  • Registration opens Monday, May 1st
  • After-school practice (returning swimmers only) May 22-24
  • New swimmer evaluations and parent meeting Tuesday, May 24, 5:30 PM.
  • Regular practices every weekday morning beginning Thursday, May 25
