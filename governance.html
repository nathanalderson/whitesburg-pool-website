Governance

Whitesburg Recreation Association is a member-owned 501c-7 non-profit recreational club. WRA manages the property at 610 Sanders Rd., primarily including the swimming pool. We are owned by our members and managed by a nine-member board of directors. For any questions about leadership or governance, please email president@whitesburgpool.org.
